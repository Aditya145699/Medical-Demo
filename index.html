About Us

nalanda netralaya is a leading eye care cneter in nalanda district, known for its top-quality services and caring approach. our hospital is dedicated to helping people with eye problems, using the latest technology and a team of skilled doctors and nurses.

we offer a wide range of services, from check-ups to surgeries, all in one place. our team understands that each person is different, so we create personalized treatment plans for everyone. whether it's a simple check-up or a complex surgery, we're here to help improve vision and quality of life.

we care about your comfort and security. our hospital has modern facilities and a friendly atmosphere to make you feel at ease. we want every aspect of your visit to exceed your expectation, from the waiting room to your room.

We also reach out to the community, organizing events to raise awareness about eye health and offering screenings to catch problems early. We believe in empowering people to take control of their eye health.

At Nalanda Netralaya, we're not just a hospital; we're your partner in better vision and eye care. We're proud to serve Nalanda district and beyond, making a difference in people's lives every day

Patient Rooms

Operating Rooms

Specialized rooms equipped with surgical instruments and technology for performing surgical procedures.

Emergency Department

A dedicated area equipped to handle medical emergencies, including trauma bays, treatment rooms, and triage areas.

Diagnostic Imaging

Facilities for various imaging tests such as X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, ultrasound, and mammography.

Laboratories

Spaces equipped for analyzing blood, urine, and other bodily fluids, as well as conducting tests such as blood typing, cultures, and pathology examinations.

Pharmacy

An area where medications are prepared, dispensed, and managed, often including inpatient and outpatient pharmacy services.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Specialized units for critically ill patients requiring close monitoring and advanced medical care.

Rehabilitation Facilities

Spaces for physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and other forms of rehabilitation to help patients recover and regain function.

Maternity Ward

Facilities for prenatal care, labor and delivery, postpartum care, and neonatal intensive care for newborns.

Cafeteria/Cafes

Dining facilities where patients, visitors, and staff can purchase meals and refreshments.

Chapel/Prayer Room

A quiet space for meditation, prayer, or religious services, catering to the spiritual needs of patients and visitors.

Administrative Offices

Offices for hospital administration, including management, billing, medical records, and human resources.

Waiting Areas

Spaces for patients and visitors to wait before appointments, tests, or procedures, often equipped with seating, reading materials, and sometimes TVs or play areas for children.

Parking

On-site parking facilities for patients, visitors, and staff, often including designated areas for disabled parking.

Security
